HEART (Human Embodiment and Reflexive Technology): About the artist  

Maja Bay (she/they) is a Danish designer, currently doing her bachelor's degree at Design Academy Eindhoven. In her practice she works across different disciplines – with a strong focus on digital and textile materials. Combining a love for thorough research with an injection of humor and play. These qualities intertwine and create a framework for sharing, interaction and understanding.
